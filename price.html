What is the difference between a proxy and a VPN?

There are a lot of things to mention here. Basically, a proxy is used mostly in browsers and other applications which can be configured to use them. The traffic for that specific software is routed through the proxy and appears to be comming from it instead of your real ip address. The VPN routes all your internet traffic system wide through our servers which makes it more powerful and safer given the fact that all our VPNs are running on 128-bit encrypted tunnels.
